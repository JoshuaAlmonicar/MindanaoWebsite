Higalaay Festival in Cagayan de Oro

The Higalaay Festival known as the Friendship Festival is held in the month of August at Cagayan de Oro City known as the City of Golden Friendship, the festival commemorates the feast day of the city's patron saint, Saint Augustine of Hippo the festival spans throughout August with a myriad of activities at the festival such as the carnival parade, fireworks display, sporting events and religious processions.

How to get there

Domestic flights to Laguindingan Airport, the main airport of Cagayan de Oro City are available from Manila, Cebu and Davao via Cebu Pacific and Philippine Airlines which have direct flights to Laguindingan from Manila and Cebu.

What to expect

Much like the other festivals such as Tnalak and Zamboanga Hermosa, there will be a calendar of events published by the city officials, make sure to inquire about the events prior to the festival so you may plan your journey.
