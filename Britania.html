Britania Group of Islands

The Britania group of islands consists of 24 islets scattered throughout Lianga Bay in the Pacific Ocean, each islets comes with various forms and sizes and can be reached by boats as island hopping is one of the most popular activities in Lianga Bay

How to get there

From Bancasi Airport in Butuan City:
Take a domestic flight to Bancasi Airport In Butuan City, commuter buses and vans will take you directly to the closest town, San Augustin, it is a 4-5 hours trip from the airport. Once at San Augistin, hire a tricycle to Britania Port.

Island Hopping

There are a number of agencies at Britania port that rent their boats, hiring prices for boats that can fir up to 15 people can range from 1500-2000 pesos, smaller boats range from 300-600 pesos. We recommend Arena Blanca Resort as they offer better deals with boat renting as well as accommodation.
