Zamboanga Hermosa Festival

In Western Mindanao, Zamboanga City holds an annual festival at the beginning October known as the "Fiesta Pillar" is celebrated in honour of the city's patroness whom had left behind a cultural legacy after 300 years of Spanish colonial rule and held as a unifying cultural and historical symbol. The month long festivities hosts a number of activities such as the "Mascota de Zamboanga" a grand parade where they a showcase a procession of intricately woven dresses and costumes where the mascot featuring the city patroness leads the procession, and "Reagatta De Zamboanga" is the infamous sailing competition where dozens of "vintas" sailing boats with colourful sails compete to race.
